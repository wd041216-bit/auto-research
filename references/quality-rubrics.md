# Quality Rubrics

## Purpose

Provide compact scoring rubrics for literature, proposals, contributions, evidence, experiments, papers, and submission readiness.

## Required Inputs

- Current workspace artifacts
- Mode
- Stage being assessed

## Required Outputs

- Scores from 1-10 with short justification
- Blocking deficiencies

## Procedure

Score only what the artifacts support. Do not infer quality from ambition or prose polish.

## Rubrics

| Rubric | 1-3 | 4-6 | 7-8 | 9-10 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Literature Quality | sparse, unverified | relevant but thin | broad and triaged | fresh, central, verified, synthesized |
| Contribution Strength | unclear | incremental or under-evidenced | clear and useful | novel, well-positioned, evidence-backed |
| Evidence Strength | anecdotal | partial support | direct support | replicated, triangulated, or formally strong |
| Experiment Readiness | no plan | plan lacks baselines or metrics | runnable with baselines | robust, reproducible, ablated |
| Paper Readiness | outline | draft with gaps | coherent and evidence-tracked | polished, reviewed, revised |
| Submission Readiness | not packageable | major artifacts missing | package mostly complete | reviewed, revised, reproducible |
| Proposal Maturity | vague idea | promising but under-specified | scored, testable, positioned | converged, veto-free, venue-ready |

## Blocking Gates

- A score cannot be above 6 if the relevant validator fails.
- Submission readiness cannot be above 6 without review and revision closure.
- Evidence strength cannot be above 6 for empirical claims without result artifacts.
- Proposal maturity cannot be `go` if any axis is below 8.
- Novelty and positioning are capped at 7 when frontier grounding is incomplete.
- Top-venue readiness is capped at 85 when evaluation design is incomplete.

## Common Failure Modes

- Giving high scores because writing is fluent.
- Scoring novelty without checking literature.
- Scoring reproducibility from descriptions rather than artifacts.
- Scoring proposal maturity without recording vetoes and gap-fill actions.
